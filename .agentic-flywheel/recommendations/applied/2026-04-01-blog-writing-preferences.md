## Trigger

- During iterative blog writing, stable preferences emerged around output language and source-link placement.

## Problem

- Those preferences were discovered during review instead of being applied in the first draft.

## Recommendation

- Store the user's stable blog-writing preferences in the harness memory so future drafts start closer to the desired style.

## Expected Benefit

- Fewer revision rounds for public technical posts.
- More consistent source attribution.

## Auto-Apply Decision

- Auto-applied because the preference is explicit, low risk, broadly reusable, and easy to reverse.
